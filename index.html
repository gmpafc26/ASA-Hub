Request Process

How Retail, Commercial and PCL appearance requests are submitted and approved

🔔 How Requests Work
Submit your request via the + New Request tab. Select TBC if you're not yet certain, or Planned if you're ready to go. You'll receive a confirmation email when submitted, and a further email once your request is approved or not approved. Once internally approved, the relevant approver will notify the football department for final sign-off.
1
Submit your request via + New Request
Fill in the request name, date, location, start time, time required, players and any relevant notes. Select your status — TBC if you’re not yet certain, or Planned if you’re ready to go.
🛍 Retail → Georgie or Andrew 💼 Commercial → Jodie or John 🌟 PCL → Jacob
2
Confirmation email sent automatically
You’ll receive an email confirming your request has been submitted and is pending review. The email includes a summary of all the details you entered.
3
Approver reviews the request
The relevant approver checks the calendar for availability and conflicts, then approves or declines the request — adding comments where needed.
🛍 Retail → Georgie or Andrew 💼 Commercial → Jodie or John 🌟 PCL → Jacob
4
Approval email sent to submitter
You’ll be notified by email of the outcome — approved or not approved — including any comments left by the approver. Status updates to Internally Approved or Not Approved.
5
Approver notifies the football department
For internally approved requests, the approver contacts the relevant football department liaison for final player confirmation. AFL requests go to Trent, AFLW to Rachael, mixed requests go to both — with players listed separately.
Do this directly from the Requests tab — click 📧 Football Dept on any Internally Approved request. Alternatively confirm offline and click ✅ Confirm to manually mark as Confirmed.
6
Confirmed — fully locked in
Once the football department has confirmed, the approver updates the status to Confirmed. The submitter receives a final email notification and the request is locked in on the calendar.
📅
Request Status Guide
  • TBC — Submitted but not yet certain. The request is in the system and pending review by the relevant approver.
  • Planned — You are certain this request needs to go ahead. Submitted and ready for the approver to review.
  • Internally Approved — Approved by the relevant approver (Retail, Commercial or PCL). The approver will now notify the football department for final sign-off.
  • Not Approved — The request was declined by the approver. Check any comments left for the reason.
  • Confirmed — Fully locked in. The football department has confirmed and the request is finalised.
Always check the calendar before submitting to avoid conflicts. You will receive email notifications at each stage of the approval process.

How It Works

Guide to using the ASA / CBA Player Requests Hub

🔒 Internal use only — do not share access or request information outside the organisation.
🤏
What is this hub?
A centralised portal for managing AFL and AFLW player appearance requests across Retail, Commercial and PCL streams. All requests, approvals, player availability and ASA/CBA balances are tracked in one place and synced live to the monday.com board.
🔐
Access levels
Anyone can view — no login needed. Approvers auto-login via their monday account.
⚪ ViewerBrowse players, calendar and requests
🟡 RetailGeorgie Morris & Andrew Fuss
🟢 CommercialJodie Cairns & John Kleeman
🌟 PCLJacob — also manages balances
🔴 AdminFull access — Georgie Morris
👥
Players tab
Browse all AFL and AFLW players with their photo, jersey number, position, ASA type and remaining balance. Toggle between squads using the AFL / AFLW buttons. Click Requests on a player card to see their upcoming bookings. Admins and approvers can edit player details directly.
📅
Calendar tab
View player availability and existing requests at a glance. Toggle between AFL, AFLW and Both views. Colour-coded bars show availability (green = available, yellow = partial, red = not available, purple = CBA). Request chips show confirmed bookings. Use + Media Blackout to block out dates when the media team is unavailable.
✉️
Making a request
Click + New Request in the top nav. Fill in the request name, type (Retail, Commercial or PCL), date, start time, duration, players and any notes. Select TBC if you are not yet certain, or Planned if you are ready to proceed. The request is automatically routed to the relevant approver once submitted.
✅
Requests & approvals
The Requests tab shows all upcoming requests sorted by date. Approvers see an amber banner when requests are pending their review. They can approve or deny with an optional comment. Approved requests move to Internally Approved — the approver then notifies the football department, after which status updates to Confirmed.
✍️
ASA / CBA balances
The Balances tab (visible to Admin and Jacob) shows each player's remaining ASA/CBA percentage as a visual bar. Jacob is responsible for keeping these up to date — enter the new percentage and click Save All to Board to sync. The last updated date is shown at the top and stamped on the board. If you believe a balance is incorrect, contact Jacob directly.
📋
monday.com board sync
Every change made in this hub — new requests, approvals, balance updates, player edits, media blackouts — is synced live to the ASA / CBA HUB board on monday.com. The board is always up to date and can be used as a source of truth for reporting or auditing.
